Why CommunityBridge

Role-based operations for therapy programs, care teams, office staff, and families.

Built for organizations that need one operational system

CommunityBridge fits programs where clinical, front-office, and family-facing work all depend on accurate schedules, clear role boundaries, and timely follow-up.

ABA therapy programs

  • Support ABA tech and BCBA workflows without losing family context
  • Track schedules, assignments, reports, and learner-specific activity in one system
  • Keep billing and authorization follow-up closer to clinical operations

Schools, clinics, and support programs

  • Centralize announcements, chats, rosters, and parent communication
  • Give office and reception staff clearer control over arrivals, scheduling, and front-desk visibility
  • Support both organization-wide and learner-level coordination

Care teams serving families

  • Give parents a cleaner experience with only the information tied to their child
  • Provide continuity across staff, families, and leadership roles
  • Support escalation with urgent communication and visible follow-up

Keep families and care teams aligned

CommunityBridge keeps parent communication connected to the same learner, roster, and schedule context used by ABA techs, BCBAs, and office teams.

Handle urgent communication with clarity

Urgent memos, alerts, and acknowledgement workflows help teams send high-priority updates with more structure than a standard chat or email thread.

Support role-based coordination

Separate experiences for parents, ABA techs, BCBAs, office, reception, and admins keep each role focused on the tools and visibility it actually needs.

Give administrators better oversight

Directories, permissions, scheduling views, reporting filters, billing exports, and operational settings give leadership clearer day-to-day visibility.

What CommunityBridge brings together

Instead of splitting communication across disconnected tools, CommunityBridge brings core coordination workflows into one system designed around real organizational roles.

Community feed and announcements

A shared home space for organization-wide updates, family communication, and team visibility.

Direct messaging and threaded chats

Built-in conversations that keep staff and families aligned without leaving the platform.

Urgent memos and acknowledgement flows

Structured, high-priority communication for time-sensitive updates.

Learner, parent, and staff directories

Directory-style views that help teams find the right contacts, relationships, and roster details quickly.

Role-based views for BCBA, ABA tech, office, reception, admin, and parent workflows

Experiences designed so each audience sees only the tools relevant to its scope and permissions.

Admin controls for permissions, alerts, settings, and exports

Operational oversight tools for leadership, office, and other authorized admin users.

Scheduling and staff assignment workflows

Calendar-based scheduling with learner session updates and staff assignment actions from the same workspace.

Reports, authorizations, and billing exports

Clinical and operational reporting plus billing and authorization workflows that support office follow-through.
