CommunityBridge Organization Intake

Submit your organization for approval.

Complete this intake once for your organization and locations. After review, CommunityBridge will activate your organization, programs, and campuses so users can join with the approved enrollment codes immediately.

What happens next

  • Your submission is stored for review.
  • An approval email is sent to CommunityBridge operations.
  • When approved, your organization and locations are written directly into the app.
  • Your users can join using the approved ZIP or enrollment codes tied to each location.

Organization

Enter the main organization or headquarters information exactly as it should appear in CommunityBridge.

Primary Contact

This is the person CommunityBridge will follow up with if onboarding questions come up.

Programs and Campuses

Add every program/location pair that should be active after approval.

Notes

Optional rollout details, implementation notes, or anything you want included in the approval review.
